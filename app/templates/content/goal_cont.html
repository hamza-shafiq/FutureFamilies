GOAL

This company has been organized specifically for the benefit of infants and young children everywhere.

Our goal is Children’s Kindergarten Readiness and future success. We seek to substantially increase these for all children and especially for those most in need. We’ll achieve this by helping prepare parents for their exceedingly important role and by informing all of the importance of parenting and CKR.

The immediate objective of our work is to understand the efficacy of teaching parenting and child development to adolescents, and if efficacious, to investigate opportunities for its implementation.

We are wholly science based and research driven in pursuing these goals.
